M U Ĩ N O

Mediterranean & Moorish inspired tapas and tasty drinks right on your doorstep.

Muiño - meaning 'mill' in Gallego - is a family run business bringing you the perfect relaxed atmosphere for sharing great food and drink. Inspired by the relaxed culture of Galicia in the Northwest of Spain but using the best of local South West produce we are aiming to provide the perfect place to hang out.

Delicious sharing plates just around the corner. Whether it’s our tapas with a twist in the evening, or a luxurious Saturday lunch, we’ve got you covered.

Our wines, beers, cocktails and coffee have all been selected carefully to ensure a great all round experience.
